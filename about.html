About Us

Capturing Your Love Story, One Frame at a Time

Capturing Emotions. Crafting Moments.

Your Story Deserves to Be Told Beautifully

WhiteTake Films is a premier wedding photography and cinematography studio based in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. We specialize in capturing genuine emotions and timeless moments through candid storytelling and cinematic visuals, creating wedding memories that feel natural, elegant, and unforgettable.

With years of experience photographing weddings across Kerala and beyond, our passionate team understands the cultural richness and emotional depth of every celebration. From intimate engagements to grand receptions, we blend artistry with technical excellence to deliver stunning wedding photography, pre-wedding shoots, and cinematic wedding films.

At WhiteTake Films, we believe every love story is unique. That's why we take a personalized approach, ensuring your wedding day is documented authentically—preserving the laughter, tears, and joy for generations to come. Trust us to be part of your special day and let us turn your moments into masterpieces.
