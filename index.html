About us

Based in Trivandrum and traveling India-wide, WhiteTake Films is a premium wedding photography and filmmaking team. Trusted by 250+ couples across 300+ events we craft cinematic films, artistic photography, and luxury albums. Combining candid emotions with grand celebrations, we are known for professionalism, personalized care, and fast, reliable delivery turning your once-in-a-lifetime moments into timeless stories.
